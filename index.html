Xuejun Richard Li, Professor, Department of Neurosurgery, Xiangya Hospital, from Liuyang, Hunan. Member of the Communist Party of China, Doctor of Medicine, Chief Physician, Deputy Director of the Department of Neurosurgery, Xiangya Hospital, Doctoral Supervisor, Postdoctoral Cooperative Supervisor, Visiting Professor of Stanford University, Hunan Brain Tumor International Director of Research Center, Deputy Director of Hunan Skull Base Surgery and Neurotumor Clinical Medical Technology Research Center, Director of International Exchange and Cooperation Office of Xiangya Hospital of Central South University.

Research interests

The main research directions are skull base, neurotumor clinical and basic research, development of intelligent diagnosis system based on central nervous system tumor molecular target research and big data machine learning, neurotumor epigenetics, artificial intelligence imaging diagnosis, etc.
